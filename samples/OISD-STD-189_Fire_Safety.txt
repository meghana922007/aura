OISD STANDARD - 189: ROTATING MACHINERY COMPLIANCE
===================================================
Identifier: OISD-STD-189-SAFETY
Authority: Oil Industry Safety Directorate

Section 4.2: Earthing and Grounding Safety Rules:
- Stator, frame, and motor casings of rotating machinery (Pumps P-102, Compressors C-302) must have dual grounding copper braids.
- CASING RESISTANCE: Max grounding resistance must check below 5.0 Ohms at all times. Inspections are scheduled every 90 days.

Section 8.5: Extinguishers Compliance:
- Fire safety equipment, CO2, and dry chemical powder extinguishers in mechanical rooms must be recertified, weight checked, and tagged every 180 days.
- Overdue extinguishers constitute a class-A regulatory audit violation.
